Quitify Account Deletion Request

To delete your account and associated data from the Quitify app, please follow the steps below:

Contact for Account Deletion Request

To delete your account, please send an email to swankydata9@gmail.com. Make sure to specify in your email that you are requesting to delete your account.

Data to be Deleted

The following data will be deleted:

  • Your name
  • Your smoking habits
  • Data related to your smoking cessation process

Data Retention Period

Your data may be retained for up to 5 days after your deletion request and will then be permanently deleted.
