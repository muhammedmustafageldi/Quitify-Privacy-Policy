Privacy Policy for Quitify by Swanky

Quitify is developed by Swanky to support users in their journey to quit smoking. We take your privacy seriously and have implemented necessary measures to protect your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected within Quitify.

1. Information Collected

When using Quitify, we may collect the following data:

  • Personal Information: Name, quit date, daily cigarette consumption, number of cigarettes in a pack, pack price, currency, and smoking history duration.
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and session details.
  • Backup Information: Data stored in Firebase Firestore for backup and restore purposes. This includes Room database files uploaded at user-defined intervals (e.g., 3 days, 1 week, 1 month).
  • Advertising Data: Non-personalized ad data for displaying general ads via Google AdMob.

2. Use of Information

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To track and display progress in quitting smoking.
  • To securely back up and restore user data using Firebase Firestore.
  • To display non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data shared with Firebase Firestore is for backup purposes only and remains private.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose. Backup data in Firebase can be deleted upon user request. Non-backup data is retained locally and is not shared externally.

5. Data Security

We ensure your data is securely stored and protected from unauthorized access. Firebase Firestore uses advanced security measures to protect your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Quitify is not designed for children under 13 years of age, and no personal information is knowingly collected from them.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Modify or delete your personal data. Contact us at swankydata9@gmail.com to request data deletion.

8. Google AdMob

Quitify uses Google AdMob for non-personalized advertisements. Users can manage or opt out of personalized ads via Google settings. For more information about Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

9. Internet Permissions

Quitify requires internet access solely for Firebase Firestore backups and Google AdMob advertisements. No additional permissions are requested.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
